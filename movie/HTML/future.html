Interactive Documentary

Future

Immersive experience and interactivity

  • VR (Virtual Reality)

    1，High immersion: VR technology can fully immerse viewers in the world of the documentary, offering a 360-degree panoramic view that makes viewers feel like they are part of the experience.

    2，Interactive participation: Viewers can freely explore the virtual environment, choose different perspectives and paths, increasing their engagement with the story.

  • MR (Mixed Reality)

    1，Virtual-real integration: MR combines virtual content with the real world and enables viewers to experience enhanced interactive content in a real environment.

    2，Augmented reality experience: Viewers can interact with virtual objects and see additional information and animations, enriching the expression of the documentary.

Project Syria utilizes state-of-the-art Virtual Reality technologies to immerse the audience in the experiences of Syrian children during the civil war, providing a deeply emotional and realistic perspective.

personalized and intelligent content

Artificial Intelligence

Personalized recommendations: AI tailors content and viewing paths based on viewers' interests and behaviors, enhancing engagement.

Intelligent Interaction

Emotion recognition: AI identifies viewers' emotions via facial expressions & voice analysis, adjusting narratives for deeper emotional resonance.

HIGHRISE

"UNIVERSE WITHIN" web doc showcases digital lives of urban dwellers. Guided by avatars, users journey from Baku to Guangzhou, reflecting global connections.
